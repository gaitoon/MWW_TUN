HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE
Get as close to 51 points as possible without going over. If you reach 51 or more, you lose 1 point.
CARD VALUES
7, 8 — Face value (7 or 8 pts)
9 — 0 points
10 — +10 or -10 (you choose)
J — Reverses turn order
Q — 3 points
K — 4 points
A — 1 or 11 (you choose)
HOW TO PLAY
1. Click a card to select it (it rises up)
2. Click again to deselect
3. Click THROW CARD to play it
4. For Ace or 10, choose value first
5. Turn passes to the next player
WINNING
Play multiple rounds. Highest score at the end wins. Avoid going over 51 to keep your points.
TURN ORDER
Bottom (YOU) — Top — Right — Left (anti-clockwise)
OBJECTIVE
Capture cards from the table by matching or summing them to your card. Score the most points to win.
THE DECK
40 cards — A 2 3 4 5 6 7 J Q K in 4 suits. No 8, 9, 10.
HOW TO CAPTURE
Play a card that MATCHES a table card (e.g. 7 captures 7)
OR play a card that SUMS with multiple table cards (e.g. 7 captures 3+4)
If both match and sum exist, you must take the MATCH
If no capture is possible, your card STAYS on the table
CHKOBBA
If you clear ALL cards from the table — that is a CHKOBBA! +1 bonus point each time.
SCORING (per round)
Most cards captured — +1 pt
Most diamonds captured — +1 pt
7 of diamonds — +1 pt
BERMILA (most 6s and 7s) — +1 pt
Each CHKOBBA — +1 pt
GOAL
Be the first player to get rid of all your cards.
ON YOUR TURN
1. Draw 1 card from the pile or discard
2. Play cards if you can
3. Discard 1 card
VALID PLAYS
Sequence: 3 or more cards in order, same suit
Example: 5♥ 6♥ 7♥
Set: 3 or 4 cards of the same number
Example: 9♠ 9♦ 9♣
OPENING (51)
Before you can play on the table, you must open with at least 51 points.
CARD VALUES:
2–10 = Face value
J, Q, K = 10
Ace = 1 or 11
Joker = value of card it replaces
AFTER OPENING
You can:
• Add cards to your melds
• Add cards to other melds on the table
JOKER RULE
If a meld has a Joker and you have the real card, you can replace the Joker and take it into your hand.
WINNING
The first player to empty all cards wins.
SCORING
• If you opened and lost → your score = cards left in hand
• If you didn't open → 100 points
• Joker = 20 points
